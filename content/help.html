What is Join?

Join is a project management tool that helps individuals and teams manage their schedules and notes. It provides an easy-to-use notepad-like interface for creating and editing notes, memos, emails, messages, shopping lists, and to-do lists. With Join, users can prioritize and set deadlines for tasks, making it a valuable tool for staying organized and on top of important tasks.

How to use it

1.  Sign up for an account on our website.
2.  Once you're logged in, you'll be taken to your dashboard where you can create a new project and start adding tasks.
3.  To add a task, click on the "Add Task" button on your dashboard. From there, you can add a title, description, and set a due date and priority level for the task.
4.  You can also view and manage your tasks on our customizable task boards, which allow you to view tasks by project, priority, or due date.
5.  Collaborate with your team members by sharing your task boards and assigning tasks to specific team members.
6.  Use our calendar view to easily schedule tasks and keep track of important deadlines.
7.  You can also access Join from your mobile device, allowing you to stay productive on-the-go.
